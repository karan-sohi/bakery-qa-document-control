SOP-QA-014 Allergen Changeover Verification

Document Type: SOP
Current Version: v4.0
Effective Date: Jun 14, 2026
Review Due: Jun 28, 2026
Department: Sanitation
Approved By: QA Manager

Purpose

Define the required verification steps after allergen changeover cleaning for bakery production lines, utensils, and adjacent staging areas.

Procedure

  1. Confirm previous production run and allergen profile before line release.
  2. Complete visual inspection of belts, hoppers, trays, scales, racks, and food-contact tools.
  3. Record swab results or QA verification initials where required by the product matrix.
  4. Escalate any residue, unlabeled ingredient, or incomplete cleaning record to QA before restart.

Records

Attach the completed sanitation checklist and retain the approved record according to the QA retention schedule.

SOP-QA-014 Allergen Changeover Verification

Document Type: SOP
Current Version: v4.0
Effective Date: Jun 14, 2026
Review Due: Jun 28, 2026
Department: Sanitation
Approved By: QA Manager

Scope

This SOP applies to production lines, sanitation tools, ingredient staging zones, packaging contact surfaces, and shared utensils used after allergen-containing products.

Verification Checklist

  1. Line clearance completed and documented.
  2. Food-contact surfaces inspected with no visible residue.
  3. QA swab or verification record attached when required.
  4. Supervisor and QA initials completed before release.

Revision Notes

v4.0 adds staging-area verification and clarifies swab documentation requirements for high-risk allergen transitions.
